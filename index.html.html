Privacy Policy

App: Kids Learn & Play (package com.Kid_basics.myapp)
Effective date: 19 June 2026

Kids Learn & Play ("the app", "we") is an educational game for young children. We have designed it to be safe and private by default. This policy explains, in plain language, exactly what data the app does and does not handle.

Summary

  • We do not collect any personal information.
  • We do not require an account or sign-in.
  • The app has no internet permission and makes no network requests.
  • There are no ads and no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • There are no in-app purchases.
  • No data is shared with us or any third party.

Information we do NOT collect

The app does not collect, transmit, or store on any server: names, email addresses, phone numbers, photos, contacts, location, device identifiers, advertising IDs, or any other personal or sensitive information from you or your child.

Information stored on the device only

To remember a child's learning progress, the app saves a small amount of non-personal data locally on the device using the operating system's standard local storage:

  • Stars and badges earned, and level/module completion.
  • Chosen settings (selected language, and whether music, sound effects and voice narration are on or off).

This information never leaves the device, is not linked to any identity, and is removed when you uninstall the app or use the in-app "Reset progress" option in the grown-ups area.

Voice narration

The app speaks words and instructions using your device's built-in text-to-speech engine. This processing happens on the device. The app does not record audio and does not use the microphone.

Permissions

The app requests no sensitive permissions. It does not use the internet, camera, microphone, location, contacts, or storage of personal files.

Children's privacy

This app is intended for children and is designed to comply with children's privacy rules including the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the EU GDPR (including provisions for children), and Google Play's Families policy. Because the app collects no personal data and shows no ads, it does not knowingly gather information from children or anyone else. Parental controls (stars, settings and reset) are placed behind a simple grown-ups gate.

Third parties

We do not share data with third parties because we do not collect any. The app includes no advertising networks, no analytics, and no social features. (Note: if you install the app from Google Play, Google may collect limited information as described in Google's own policies; that is outside our control and not initiated by the app.)

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the effective date above. Material changes will be reflected before they take effect.
